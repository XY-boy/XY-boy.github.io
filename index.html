Biography

Yi Xiao is a Ph.D. candidate at Wuhan University, under the supervision of Prof. Qiangqiang Yuan (袁强强) and Liangpei Zhang (张良培). He received his B.S. degree from the China University of Geoscience and Huazhong University of Science and Technology and his M.S. degree from Wuhan University. His research interests include remote sensing image/video processing and computer vision, mainly focusing on super-resolution and low-level vision tasks.
